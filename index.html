Map Reading

Don't get left behind.

Introduction

This website contains information on how to read a map. By the end of this course, students will be able to read maps, follow directions and identify and efficient route between two places.

Course Learning Objectives

  1. Students will demonstrate the ability to identify the cardinal directions on a compass rose.
  2. Students will demonstrate the ability to follow directions on a map to reach a destination.
  3. Students will demonstrate the ability to identify an efficient route between two points on a map.
